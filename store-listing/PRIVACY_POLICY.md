# Privacy Policy — Dharma: Daily Wisdom

**Last updated: March 26, 2026**

## Overview

Dharma ("the App") is developed and maintained by DharmaWeave (Tara Ventures). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking
- Does NOT transmit data to any server
- Does NOT require an internet connection to function
- Does NOT use cookies or advertising identifiers
- Does NOT access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location

## Local Storage

The App stores your preferences (chosen tradition, reminder settings, learning progress) locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Notifications

If you enable daily wisdom reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device. No push notification service or external server is involved.

## Third-Party Links

The App contains links to Google Play Books and Spotify for related DharmaWeave content. These external services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, this is inherently satisfied.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
