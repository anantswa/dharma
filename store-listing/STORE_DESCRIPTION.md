## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Begin each day with sacred wisdom from five great spiritual traditions.

Dharma is a beautifully crafted spiritual companion that brings daily teachings from Hindu, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, and Zen traditions — all in one app. Whether you follow one path or draw from many, Dharma meets you where you are.

TEMPLE DARSHAN
Open the app to a digital temple experience. Swipe through stunning deity images — from Devi and Ganesha to Buddha and Mahavir — each rendered with reverence. Light the aarti, hear the sacred shankh, and start your day with devotion.

WISDOM LIBRARY
Browse 150+ teachings from the Bhagavad Gita, Guru Granth Sahib, Dhammapada, Tattvartha Sutra, and Zen masters. Each teaching includes the original transliteration and English translation. Filter by tradition to focus your practice.

SACRED CALENDAR
Never miss a festival again. 300+ sacred dates across 2026-2027 covering Hindu festivals, Sikh Gurpurabs, Buddhist observances, and Jain holy days. Tap any event to learn its significance, rituals, and spiritual meaning.

LEARN
Master the Hanuman Chalisa verse by verse with Hindi text, transliteration, and English meaning. Bhagavad Gita lessons coming soon.

DEVOTIONAL MUSIC
Built-in Om chanting, shankh sounds, and devotional tracks for meditation and prayer.

DAILY REMINDERS
Set a gentle morning reminder to receive a wisdom teaching from your chosen tradition.

DESIGNED WITH CARE
Dark theme with saffron-gold accents, Playfair Display typography, and glass-morphism cards create a serene, premium experience.

OFFLINE-FIRST
Everything works without internet. Your spiritual practice is never interrupted.

MULTI-TRADITION
Equal respect for all traditions. Hindu, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, and Zen — choose one or embrace all.

This app is a living project by DharmaWeave. We welcome feedback and corrections. Content is updated over time to serve practitioners better.
